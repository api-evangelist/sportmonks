An odds movement tracker is a system that monitors how bookmaker odds for a fixture change over time, from the opening line through kick-off and across the in-play window, surfacing significant shifts that may indicate sharp money, breaking team news, or market realignment. The article walks through designing such a tracker using the Sportmonks API.
