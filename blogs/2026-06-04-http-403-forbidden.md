An HTTP 403 Forbidden error indicates the server recognized your identity but denied access to the requested resource, typically due to subscription limitations. The article distinguishes 403 from 401 errors and outlines common causes including accessing endpoints or leagues outside your plan, IP restrictions, and stale subscriptions. Solutions involve upgrading subscriptions or adding required add-ons through account management.
